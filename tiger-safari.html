Ranthambore Tiger Safari

Discover the attractions of the amazing Ranthambore Tiger Reserve, where the fascinating Bengal Tiger roams free in its native environment. Experience the distinct appeal of this reserve, which provides a one-of-a-kind chance to see the Bengal Tiger in all of its magnificent glory.

If you’re looking for information on how to book a Ranthambore safari online, you’ve come to the right place. At Roar & Safari, we offer easy booking options through our website, phone calls, and WhatsApp.

Ranthambore National Park & Tiger Reserve provides two types of jungle safaris: Jeep Safari and Canter Safari. It’s crucial to book your seats in advance for both options, and our website simplifies this process by collecting the necessary details. The entire procedure, from vehicle allocation to crowd management, is overseen by the forest authority of Ranthambore Tiger Reserve. To ensure smooth tiger safaris, the national park is divided into ten zones, and safari arrangements are made in available zones based on availability. Additionally, Ranthambore online safaris are accessible for each of these zones.

Now, let’s explore the two safari vehicle options in Ranthambore National Park:

Jeep Safari:

Jeep safaris are highly sought after in Ranthambore, but securing a spot can be challenging due to limited jeep availability. Jeep safaris accommodate only six passengers, making them ideal for small families and groups of friends. Booking a jeep safari in the current and tatkal quota can be competitive and more expensive than canter safaris. We offer two types of jeep safari bookings: advance Jeep Booking, which allows reservations up to 12 months in advance, and tatkal Jeep safari booking, offering exclusive jeep bookings for individuals or small groups.

Canter Safari:

The Canter Safari provides a more accessible and economical way to explore Ranthambore’s wilderness. With a seating capacity of 20, it’s suitable for larger groups, whether you’re traveling with family, friends, or colleagues. Canter safaris offer more availability and flexibility. You can book Canter safaris in advance to secure your spot and have a better chance of getting your preferred safari zone. However, in cases where advance quotas are not available, we cannot guarantee a specific zone. Safaris can be booked for any of the ten zones, each offering a unique wildlife experience.

Things to Keep in Mind for Ranthambore Safari Booking:
  • Payment of the full safari fees in advance is mandatory.
  • Every visitor must present a valid ID proof.
  • Confirmed bookings are non-refundable and cannot be canceled.
  • Bookings are non-transferable and cannot be swapped with others.
  • Ensure you carry the same ID card submitted during online booking.
  • Safari reservations can be made up to 365 days in advance.
  • Foreign tourists must provide passport details for their reservations.
  • Safari permits are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis, subject to availability.
  • Visitors will be responsible for paying any fee differences in case of revisions after booking, upon entry into the park.
  • Prior to the safari, guests can opt for a choice jeep (modified luxury premium glass vehicle with an experienced driver) for an additional fee payable to the forest department.
  • Guests also have the option to select an experienced tour guide for their safari, with extra charges payable to the forest department.
