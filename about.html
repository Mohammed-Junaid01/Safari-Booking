About Ranthambhore Safari

Embark on an exhilarating wildlife adventure at Ranthambore National Park, nestled in the heart of India's untamed wilderness. This renowned sanctuary offers a unique opportunity to witness Bengal tigers in their natural habitat. Traverse the diverse terrain, ranging from arid forests to tranquil lakes, aboard canter and jeep safaris. Ranthambore's abundant biodiversity encompasses not only majestic tigers but also elusive leopards and a myriad of avian species. To guarantee an unforgettable wildlife experience, it is highly recommended to secure your entry permits in advance, as they are limited. Immerse yourself in the wonders of Ranthambore National Park, where every moment promises a thrilling encounter with the wild.

About Ranthambhore

Explore the wild beauty and regal appeal of Ranthambore National Park, which is tucked away in the centre of Rajasthan. For lovers of wildlife, this famous sanctuary offers an exciting retreat where wild tigers roam free and beautiful landscapes blend together to form a breathtaking tapestry of natural beauties.

Leopards move stealthily through the thick vegetation of Ranthambore, lending an air of mystery to the rich variety that appears. The sound of history reverberates from the park's historic fort, which watches over the peaceful coexistence of heritage and nature. Make the most of your trip in Ranthambore by immersing yourself in this engrossing tale of the majesty of nature and its historical past.
